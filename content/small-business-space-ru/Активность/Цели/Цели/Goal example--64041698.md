# Goal example

Period: Ежегодно
Timeline: January 1, 2020 → January 1, 2021
Difficulity: Hard
Value: Low
Reached: No

**S. Specific**

---

- What do you want to accomplish?
- Why do you want to accomplish it?
- What are the requirements?
- What are the constraints?

**M. Measurable**

---

- How will I measure my progress?
- How will I know when the goal is accomplished?

**A. Achievable**

---

- How can the goal be accomplished?
- What are the logical steps I should take?

**R. Relevant**

---

- Is this a worthwhile goal?
- Is this the right time?
- Do I have the necessary resources to accomplish this goal?
- Do these goals line up with long-term objectives?

**T. Time-bound**

---

- How long will it take to accomplish this goal?
- When is the completion of this goal due?
- When I'm going to work on this goal?
