# Lifecycle Stages

Lifecycle stages in eCommerce mainly refers to the stages a product undergoes before becoming mature. It is a course of events that brings a new product into existence by growing through the main 5 phases namely:
1) Development Phase
2) Market Introduction Phase
3) Growth Phase
4) Maturity Phase
5) Decline/Stability Phase
The Lifestyle stages include firstly market analysis and product development, after that release of product with high level advertising, then the eventual sales rise, thus reaching the upper bounds of demand cycle and gradually declining due to introduction of newer products.
