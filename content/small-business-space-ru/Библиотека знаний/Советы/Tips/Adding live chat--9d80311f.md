# Adding live chat

Using [live chat](https://acquire.io/live-chat/), customers can ask questions or express concerns before making any buying decisions, thus increasing your conversion rate and creating a great shopping experience for your brand.

![](https://acquire.io/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/live-chat.png)

[Offering live chat on your site helps you discover common problems experienced by your customers.](http://ctt.ec/9a6PZ)

Resolving those problems helps improve your conversions.

[A BoldChat survey found that 31 percent of the online shoppers in the U.S. and UK are more likely to buy after utilizing the live chat feature on a site.Click To Tweet](http://ctt.ec/JAK9z)

![](https://acquire.io//wp-content/uploads/2016/08/uploads/imported/live-chat-experience_48005637.jpg)

Source: [Econsultancy](https://econsultancy.com/consumers-prefer-live-chat-for-customer-service-stats/)

There are many reasons customers appreciate live chat, and time is one of them.

Of those who agree live chat is the most effective communication method, 79 percent said they did so because of quick responses.
