# Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

This is referred to as a Predictable Revenue! A revenue/income that any business is liable to receive every month consistently, is termed as MRR – Monthly Recurring Revenue.
This acts as a by-product of a subscription business which implies billing takes place on monthly basis. The simple way to calculate your MRR is to multiply your total number of paying users by the Average Revenue Per User (ARPU).
