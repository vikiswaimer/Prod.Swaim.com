# Ensure excellent customer support

The [ecommerce](https://www.geekschip.com/blog/importance-of-digital-marketing-for-ecommerce-business) marketplace isn’t only about selling quality products and creating must-have designs. Obviously, these help, but to really stand out in a competitive market you must provide excellent customer support. This will determine the sustainability of your business.

According to [one study:](https://www.talkdesk.com/blog/10-customer-services-statistics-for-call-center-supervisors/)

[78% of consumers pulled out of an intended purchase due to low or poor customer service](http://ctt.ec/B2cN0)

So, if you don’t provide [**great customer service**](https://acquire.io/blog/key-considerations-improve-customer-support/), you may lose potential customers.

Providing the best customer service means being available multi-channel:

- Social media
- Email
- [Live chat](https://acquire.io/blog/live-chat-best-customer-service/)
- Phone
- Customer service center or help desk

Your customers need to be able to reach you through these mediums and will expect a swift response.
