# Reach Out to Customers in Their Native Languages

It’s common for ecommerce stores to sell items to people all over the world. Just because you speak English doesn’t mean that you can’t sell a hat to someone who speaks Portuguese.

Reach out to foreign customers by providing translation features to your online store. Most ecommerce platforms, [WordPress ecommerce](https://ecommerce-platforms.com/articles/7-best-free-wordpress-ecommerce-plug-ins) themes, and [WooCommerce](https://ecommerce-platforms.com/compare/shopify-vs-woocommerce-comparison) templates provide translation settings so that you don’t exclude people in other countries who don’t understand your language.
