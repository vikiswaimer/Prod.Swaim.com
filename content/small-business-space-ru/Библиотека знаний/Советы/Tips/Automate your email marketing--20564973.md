# Automate your email marketing

Automation is essential for [boosting your email marketing](https://esputnik.com/en/blog/5-tips-how-use-email-automation-improve-conversion-rates). Using pre-set triggers allows you to send hyper-relevant and timely messages seamlessly, delighting your subscribers.

![](https://acquire.io/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Setup-automated-email-reports-in-Google-Analytics.jpg)

According to Mailgen, [89 percent](https://www.mailigen.com/blog/infographic-segmented-emails-is-what-the-future-holds/) of marketers said that email is their key channel for lead generation.

It’s well worth the effort to be more proactive and automate your email marketing campaigns for a smoother and more effective process. For instance, if a customer abandons his cart, an email can encourage him to revisit the site and complete the transaction.

Sending emails to cart abandoners is only made possible through automating your email marketing. Manually sending emails is incredibly time-consuming. Once you automate your email marketing, as soon as someone trips an online trigger, the email goes out. Statistics show that “[Automated emails get 119 percent higher click rates than broadcast emails.](https://zephoria.com/triggered-message-click-rates-over-119-percent-higher-than-normal-business-email/)” and B2C marketers who used automated emails noticed conversion rates as high as 50 percent.
